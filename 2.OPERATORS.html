3.>>>

Signed Right Shift (>>):
Keeps the sign bit (leftmost bit). For -8, the sign bit remains 1 after shifting.
Unsigned Right Shift (>>>):
Does not preserve the sign bit. Fills the leftmost bits with 0 regardless of the sign.



NOTES

1.if the number is negative to preserve the sign the left and right shift operator fills the empty places with 1
2.if the number is positive to preserve the sign the left and right shift operator fills the empty places with 0
